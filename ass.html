The History of Internet

From ARPANET to the World Wide Web

The Internet began as a project of the U.S Department in the late 1920's with the creation of ARPANET, a network designed to connect various research institutions and universities

The Birth of the Web

In the 1980's, British computer scientist Tim Berners-Lee developed the first web browser and created the World Wide Web, which allowed people to access information and communicate with each other over the Internet.

The Dot-com Boom

In the 1990's, the Internet exploded in popularity with the rise of e-commerce and the dot-com boom.Companies like Amazon ,ebay and Google emerged as major players in the online world.

The Mobile Revolution

In the 2000s and 2010s, the Internet continued to evolve with advent of smartphones and mobile devices, making it possible for people to access the web from anywhere at any time

The Future of the Internet

As technology continues to advance, it is difficult to predict what the future of the Internet will look like, but it's clear that the Internet will continue to play a major role in shaping our world.
